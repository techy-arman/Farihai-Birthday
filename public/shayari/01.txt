Kya ajooba hain aap, ye aapko koi nahi batayega,
Aapko ek nazar dekh le jo, seedha ishq mein pad jayega.
Aur log kehte hain ki chaand bahut khoobsurat hai,
Lekin chaand ka kya muqabla aapse...
Woh aap jaisi aankhen kahan se laayega?
Aur kisi ka koi muqabla nahi aapse,
Kyunki aapki woh haseen muskurahat...
Koi saat janmon mein bhi na laa payega. ✨🤍
