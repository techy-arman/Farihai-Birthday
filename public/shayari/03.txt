Aaj ka din bas ek tareekh nahi hai,
Aaj ka din ek khoobsurat kahani ka jashn hai.
Ek aisi rooh ka jashn,
Jo apni saadgi se dil jeet leti hai.
Ek aisi muskurahat ka jashn,
Jo udaas din ko bhi haseen bana deti hai.
Aur ek aise insaan ka jashn,
Jise dekh kar lagta hai ki duniya ab bhi khoobsurat hai. ❤️✨
