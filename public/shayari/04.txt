Har sitara aaj aasman mein chamak raha hoga,
Har phool aaj thoda aur mehak raha hoga.

Shayad sabko pata chal gaya hai,
Ki aaj ek bahut khoobsurat din hai.

Kyunki aaj us shakhs ka janamdin hai,
Jiski saadgi mein bhi ek alag si baat hai,

Aur jise dekh kar lagta hai,
Ki khoobsurti sirf chehre mein nahi, rooh mein bhi hoti hai. ✨❤️
